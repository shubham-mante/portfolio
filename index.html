About Me

I’m an Embedded Systems Engineer who enjoys building connected devices from the ground up — firmware, hardware bring-up, wireless connectivity, RTOS design (Zephyr / FreeRTOS), power optimization, security, and production readiness.

Over the period, I’ve worked across medical devices, industrial IoT, and smart-energy systems, designing firmware for Nordic, ESP32, and STM32 platforms and integrating wireless technologies like BLE, Wi-Fi, MQTT, LoRaWAN, NFC, LTE and NB-IoT.

I’m especially passionate about the full lifecycle of embedded product development — from early architecture and prototype firmware to secure boot, OTA updates, and compliance testing (FCC, SAR, IEC 60601).

I enjoy solving the hard problems : flaky radios, unstable boards, timing bottlenecks, power-hungry peripherals, or debugging the misbehaving RTOS threads.
